Cricket

Cricket is a popular bat-and-ball game played between two teams of 11 players. It's especially popular in countries like India, England, and Australia.

Rules of Cricket

  • Each team has 11 players.
  • The game is played in innings (usually 1 or 2).
  • Runs are scored by batsmen running between wickets or hitting boundaries.
  • A team is out when 10 wickets fall.
  • Bowling, batting, and fielding roles are rotated.
